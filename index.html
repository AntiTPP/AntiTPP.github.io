This is the Real TPP

The TPP is a trade agreement constantly hailed as a great boost to businesses. But how does it affect you?

Putting your Jobs at Stake

The TPP, according to a study at Tufts' Global Development and Environment Institute, will directly lead to 448,000 Americans losing their jobs.

Greater Income For Everyone - Or Not

For those who still have their jobs, the TPP will help raise their income - if it's already over $88,000. Is this an income boost or a booster for wage inequality?

Cheaper Medicine - at a Cost

The TPP offers many different things, such as cheaper medicine made possible by lax trade agreements. However, this comes at the cost of little to no regulation from the foreign entity - how do you know that the cheaper forign medicine you buy won't hurt your child instead of help them?
